Comparison of the Bubonic Plague and Measles

Similarities

  • Can induce fevers
  • They have the ability to be lethal
  • There is some sort of cure to each of the diseases
  • Both have precations to avoid catching the illness
  • Each was spread across a continent (Europe/North America)

Differences

  • The main difference is the time period (14th century compared to the 21st)
  • Modern technology to find cures and treatments (antibiotics, vaccines, pain relievers)
  • Over time, we learned how diseases and bacteria affect our health
  • Measles is a virus while the plague is caused by bacteria
  • Only 0.1-0.2% die with measles compared to the bubonic plague is 50% if untreated
